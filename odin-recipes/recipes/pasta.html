Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta (of your choice)
  • Butter (as much as you like)
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil or olive oil

Steps

  1. Add water to a pan (so that it covers the pasta when you put it in)
  2. Turn on the stove and wait until the water boils
  3. Add salt and oil/olive oil to the water
  4. Add pasta to the water
  5. Mix it from time to time to unglue the pasta and check if it's ready
  6. When it's as you like it, take the water off the pan (keep the pasta there bro)
  7. Put the pan over the stove again and add the butter
  8. Mix until the butter melts
  9. Turn off the stove and eat (duh)
