Porridge

Description

Porridge tastes good and is easy to make. It also fills you up a lot. Kinda useful for camping trips and such.

Ingredients

  • Milk (500ml)
  • Oatmeal (flakes, as much as you like)
  • Sugar (as much as you like)

Steps

  1. Pour milk on a pan
  2. Turn on the stove
  3. Put oatmeal in
  4. Put sugar in
  5. Keep mixing until it starts to boil
  6. Turn off stove
  7. Eat (duh)
