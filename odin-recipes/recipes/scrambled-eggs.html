Scrambled Eggs

Description

Simple to make, idk.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (3x)
  • Butter (as much as you like)
  • Salt (as much as you like)

Steps

  1. Put butter on frying pan
  2. Turn stove on (fire on low), let butter melt
  3. Break the eggs and put them in the pan
  4. Add salt
  5. Mix until there's no more liquid egg in the pan
  6. Turn stove off and eat (duh)
